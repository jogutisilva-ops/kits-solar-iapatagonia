# Ad Creation & Design Prompts Guide (Southern Chile Edition)

This guide provides highly optimized prompts for Gemini (copywriting) and Imagen 3 / Midjourney (visuals), tailored specifically to **Southern Chile** (Regions of Biobío, La Araucanía, Los Ríos, Los Lagos, and Chiloé) and for each of the **8 solar kits** individually.

---

## 🏔️ Background Context & Strategy for Southern Chile

When generating ad copies or visuals for Southern Chile, the AI should be fed the following background constraints:
1. **Weather Advantage (Bifacial Panels):** Southern Chile is rainier and cloudier. We use **Trina Solar 715Wp Bifacial panels** which absorb light from both sides (capturing direct sunlight from the front and diffuse light reflected from clouds, rain, or wet grass from the back). This makes solar highly viable even in the south.
2. **Grid Outages & Independence:** Winter storms and heavy rain frequently cut power in southern towns and rural fields (*parcelas/campos*). Hybrid kits with **GoodWe inverters** and **LX 5kWh/10kWh modular battery storage** provide essential continuous service backup.
3. **Local Lifestyle & Target:** People in the south value self-sufficiency, heating efficiency (switching from wood to electricity due to PDA regulations), and security for their homes, cabins (*cabañas*), farms, or tourism businesses.
4. **Terminology:** Uses Chilean Spanish (CLP pricing, *parcelas*, *cabañas*, *corte de luz*, *respaldo*, *generación distribuida*, *boleta de luz*).
